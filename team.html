Meet the Haidware Team

We’re engineers and builders focused on human‑centered IoT and AI devices. Four makers, one mission.

Adesuyi Oluwatobiloba Adekunle
Founder – CEO

Adesuyi Oluwatobiloba Adekunle is the CEO and founder of HAIDWARE. In 2023, he earned his BSc degree in Automotive engineering from Frederick university in Republic of Cyprus and started his masters in Web and Smart systems with the focus on embedded systems development. During his BSc studies, the demonstration of how to use CAN bus to communicate between different nodes, earned him an internship role with IREROBOT where he deepened his knowledge in both hardware and software development. Particularly in the realm of IoT and UNITY integration where he seamlessly established communication between ESP32 and UNITY by implementing RESTful APIs for bi-directional communication using C++ and C#. He also took part in the early development of the haptic glove. Aside all these, his versatile skill set spans across CAD design, MATLAB, web design and hands-on experience as a mechanical technician, enabling him to drive HAIDWARE's mission of creating intuitive, interconnected hardware solutions.
