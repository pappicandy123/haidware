About the device

This smart educational companion is designed for children aged 5–13 years, combining AI-powered conversations with a safe, fun, and age appropriate learning environment. Unlike traditional tablets or screens, this device uses voice interaction only with a microphone and speaker ensuring children stay engaged without screen distractions. Parents only need the app once: to connect the device to WiFi. After that, the child can use it independently.

  • Voice-only interaction encourages focus and imagination.
  • Parents connect Wi-Fi once through a simple app; kids use it independently afterward.
  • Regular cloud updates add new lessons, stories, and activities.
