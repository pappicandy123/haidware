Our partnership with Irerobot

HAIDWARE proudly partners with Irerobot, a pioneering sensory technology company specializing in cutting‑edge human–machine interaction.

Data on the Deaf population in Nigeria is fragmented and inconsistent, with estimates ranging widely depending on methodology. However, more reliable sources such as the World Health Organization indicate that the population of Deaf and hard-of-hearing Nigerians is around 12 million, representing about 5–6% of the country’s population. This highlights both the scale of the need and the significant opportunity for impactful solutions. Through our partnership with Irerobot, we are addressing this gap with the Accet device, designed to give the deaf and hard-of-hearing community a new way to interact with their surroundings

This collaboration is rooted in both shared vision and personal history: HAIDWARE’s founder worked at Irerobot in Republic of Cyprus, honing deep expertise in the hardware and robotics industry. Through this experience, HAIDWARE has built a strong foundation in IoT and AI system integration—skills now applied to create scalable, accessible, and future‑ready solutions.

Together, HAIDWARE and Irerobot bring to market breakthrough technologies that merge hardware engineering with AI‑powered intelligence, ensuring every product is both technically sound and human‑centered.
